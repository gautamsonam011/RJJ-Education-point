Welcome To R.J.J Education Point School

R.J.J Education Point School is a name that stands for its vision to provide students a modernly-designed holistic learning environment to explore, create and grow as global citizens of tomorrow.

With an aim to instill strong will-power in every child through 3C’s of life: Confidence, Compassion and Cooperation to overcome every hurdle in life with joy and togetherness.

Admission Procedure

  1. Co-educational school and follows the 10+2 pattern

  2. Admissions are open to all irrespective of caste, creed or religion

  3. At the time of admissions every student must produce a Transfer Certificate and a mark sheet from the school last attended.

  4. Registration for a particularly year is not transferable to any other year.

  5. Fees once paid are not refundable.
